IoT certified hardware

IoT devices certification guarantees that IoT devices are supported in Ubuntu releases. Our engineering teams work with IoT device vendors to enablement hardware, stabilize the core system, and verify specific applications and workloads function optimally on the platform.

Certified IoT Devices can be used by flashing a certified Ubuntu image or can come pre-installed with Ubuntu.

An IoT Device not on our list? Contact us for more information on how the IoT Device Certification program can help you.
